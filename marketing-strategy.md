# LogicWorks.AI Marketing Strategy

## Executive Summary

LogicWorks.AI is an AI-powered SEO agency that combines artificial intelligence with human expertise to deliver faster, better, and more affordable SEO services. This marketing strategy outlines how to position, promote, and grow the agency to $1M+ annual revenue.

---

## Brand Positioning

### Unique Value Proposition
**"AI-Powered SEO. Human-Grade Results."**

We use AI to deliver:
- **3x faster** project completion
- **50% lower costs** than traditional agencies
- **Transparent processes** clients can see
- **Consistent quality** through AI systems

### Target Audiences

| Segment | Description | Pain Points |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| **Small Business Owners** | Local service businesses (dentists, contractors, lawyers) | Can't afford $5K+/mo agencies; need local visibility |
| **Marketing Directors** | Mid-size companies with limited SEO budget | Need results but can't hire full team |
| **Startup Founders** | Tech/SaaS companies | Need rapid SEO growth, data-driven approach |
| **Agency Owners** | Small marketing agencies | Want to white-label SEO services |

### Competitive Positioning

| Competitor Type | Their Weakness | Our Advantage |
|-----------------|----------------|---------------|
| Big SEO Agencies | Slow, expensive, opaque | Fast, affordable, transparent |
| Freelance SEOs | Inconsistent, limited capacity | Scalable, consistent, always available |
| AI SEO Tools | No strategy, just data | Full-service with human oversight |
| DIY Business Owners | Time-consuming, learning curve | Done-for-you with explanations |

## Marketing Channels

### 1. Content Marketing (Primary)

**Blog Topics:**
- "How We Used AI to Rank #1 for 'Dental Implants Ashburn' in 90 Days"
- "The Complete Local SEO Checklist for 2026"
- "AI vs Human SEO: Why You Need Both"
- "Case Study: 300% Traffic Increase for Computer Repair Business"

**Content Formats:**
- Long-form guides (3,000+ words)
- Video walkthroughs of our dashboard
- Interactive tools (SEO score checker, ROI calculator)
- Downloadable templates (audit checklist, content calendar)

**Distribution:**
- Company blog (SEO optimized)
- LinkedIn articles
- Medium publications
- Reddit (r/SEO, r/smallbusiness)
- Indie Hackers

### 2. LinkedIn Outreach (High Priority)

**Target:** Marketing directors, business owners, agency founders

**Weekly Goals:**
- 50 personalized connection requests
- 20 meaningful comments on target posts
- 5 direct conversations started
- 1 discovery call booked

**Message Template:**
```
Hi [Name],

I help [industry] businesses get more customers from Google using AI-powered SEO.

Recently worked with [similar company] — increased their organic traffic 150% in 90 days.

Worth a 10-minute call to see if I can do the same for [Company]?

[Calendar link]
```

### 3. Local Networking (High Conversion)

**Activities:**
- Chamber of Commerce membership
- BNI (Business Network International) chapter
- Local business meetups
- Industry conferences (SMX, MozCon, LocalU)

**Goal:** 2-3 referrals per month from network

### 4. Referral Program

**Structure:**
- Current client refers new client → 1 month free SEO
- Partner agency refers client → 20% commission for 6 months

**Promotion:**
- Email to all clients
- Mention in monthly reports
- Partner outreach to marketing agencies

### 5. Paid Advertising (Scale Phase)

**Google Ads:**
- Keywords: "SEO agency [city]", "local SEO services", "AI SEO"
- Budget: $500-1,000/month initially
- Target: Landing page with ROI calculator

**LinkedIn Ads:**
- Target: Marketing directors, small business owners
- Format: Case study carousel ads
- Budget: $300-500/month

## Pricing Strategy

### Service Tiers

| Tier | Price | Best For | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|----------|
| **Starter** | $1,500 one-time | New businesses | Audit + 3-month roadmap |
| **Growth** | $2,000/month | Growing businesses | Full SEO + 4 blog posts/month |
| **Scale** | $5,000/month | Established companies | Full SEO + content + link building |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Large organizations | Dedicated AI team + strategy |

### Value-Based Positioning
- Don't compete on price
- Emphasize ROI: "$2K/month → $20K/month in new revenue"
- Show competitor pricing: "Traditional agency: $5K+/mo | Us: $2K/mo"

## Sales Process

### Discovery Call (15 min)
1. Understand their business and goals
2. Identify pain points with current SEO (if any)
3. Show dashboard demo (2 minutes)
4. Share relevant case study
5. Schedule audit or proposal

### Free SEO Audit (Lead Magnet)
- 5-page mini audit
- Delivered in 48 hours
- Includes 3 priority recommendations
- Ends with proposal

### Proposal Structure
1. Current situation analysis
2. Competitor gap analysis
3. 90-day roadmap
4. Expected results (conservative estimates)
5. Investment & next steps
